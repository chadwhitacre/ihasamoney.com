IHasAMoney.com

IHasAMoney.com is a personal finance application with the following invariants:

  • You aren’t the product.
  • Never takes your bank creds.
  • Shows data and comparisons.
  • No pie charts.
  • Nice API.
  • Lolcat.

Ways to Play

1. Play now with fake data.

2. Import your own data for free. No persistence.

3. Persist your data for $2.99/mo.

UI

Down/up arrow or j/k to select transaction.

Left/right arrow or h/l to recategorize.
